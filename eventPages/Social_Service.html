    SOCIAL SERVICE CHALLENGE - FULL DETAILS

    INTERMEDIATE LEVEL

    The "Signal-Sense" Network Mapper

    Description:A platform that intelligently connects volunteers with local community needs. Organizations post opportunities, and volunteers create profiles with their skills, interests, and availability. The AI core is a recommendation engine that suggests the most impactful and relevant opportunities.

    Requirements:

    • A dual-profile system for volunteers (skills, interests, availability) and organizations (needs, location, schedule).
    • A recommendation engine (content-based or collaborative filtering) to match volunteers with opportunities.
    • Geo-location features to prioritize local opportunities
    • A communication and scheduling system within the platform.

    Outcomes:

    • Increased volunteer participation and satisfaction by matching them with relevant opportunities.
    • More efficient fulfillment of community needs for non-profit organizations
    • A stronger, more connected local community.

    Conditions:

    • Requires building a two-sided market, attracting both a sufficient number of volunteers and organizations.
    • T○ Must ensure the legitimacy of listed organizations.

    Potential Datasets:

  • Kaggle Volunteer Opportunities Dataset: Can be used for initial analysis and building a prototype recommendation model. Link on Kaggle
    • Duration: 36 Hours
    Team Size: 1 Member
    Prize Pool: ₹40,000

    SOCIAL SERVICE CHALLENGE - FULL DETAILS

    INTERMEDIATE LEVEL

    The "Food-Flow Connector"

    Description:An app that connects restaurants and grocery stores with surplus food to nearby charities. An AI handles the logistics, figuring out the fastest routes for volunteers to pick up and drop off the food, ensuring it gets to people in need while it's still fresh.

    Key Requirements:

    • A mobile app with a simple interface for food donors to list available items and quantities.
    • A system for registered charities to view and claim available food
    • An AI-powered logistics engine to solve the Vehicle Routing Problem (VRP) for volunteer drivers, optimizing for time and freshness.
    • Real-time tracking and notification system for all parties involved

    Outcomes

    • A significant reduction in local food waste from commercial establishments.
    • An increased and more efficient supply of food for local charities and food banks.
    • An optimized and engaging platform for volunteers to contribute effectively

    Conditions:

    • Requires establishing partnerships with local food businesses, charities, and a network of volunteers
    • Must adhere to local food safety and handling regulations.
    • The logistics model depends on the real-time availability of volunteer drivers.

    Potential-dataset

    • ○ The logistics algorithm would use mapping data from services like OpenStreetMap. The operational data on food availability would be generated by the app itself.
    Duration: 36 Hours
    Team Size: 1 Member
    Prize Pool: ₹40,000
